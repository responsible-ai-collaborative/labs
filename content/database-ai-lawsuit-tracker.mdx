The **AI Lawsuit Tracker** is an independent database of AI litigation that
describes itself as the AI lawsuit tracker "of record." It monitors copyright,
privacy, defamation, antitrust, securities, BIPA, and deepfake cases — at last
count 186 lawsuits across 67 defendants in U.S., EU, and UK jurisdictions.

### Why it qualifies as a database

- **Verifiable sources** — data is pulled from PACER, state court e-filing
  systems, and primary court records, with a dedicated methodology page and
  citable statistics.
- **Actively maintained** — a live docket feed and recent rulings keep it
  current.

Explore it at [ailawsuittracker.com](https://ailawsuittracker.com/).
