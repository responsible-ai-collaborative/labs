This Springer reference-work chapter offers a broad, citable overview of how AI
risk and safety connect to the practice of **incident reporting** — why
real-world harm events are worth cataloging, how incident databases are built
and maintained, and how reporting fits into the wider landscape of AI
governance.

It is a useful entry point for readers approaching AI incident reporting for the
first time.

Read it via [Springer](https://link.springer.com/rwe/10.1007/978-981-95-3658-0_89).
