The **BakerHostetler Case Tracker** (Artificial Intelligence, Copyrights and
Class Actions) monitors key U.S. litigation raising copyright and
copyright-adjacent issues related to the creation and use of generative AI,
spanning content creators, dataset and model providers, and downstream users.

It sits in Community because the public page is largely an overview: it does not
document scope, sources, or methodology, indicate an update cadence, or provide
a way to submit corrections and additions.
