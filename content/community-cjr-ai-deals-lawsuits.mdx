The **AI Deals and Lawsuits Tracker**, from the Tow Center for Digital
Journalism at Columbia Journalism Review, monitors developments between news
publishers and AI companies — including lawsuits, commercial deals, and grants —
based on publicly available information.

It is a valuable, reputable resource, but it sits in Community for now: it ranges
beyond AI harms and litigation into commercial deals, and the page does not
document a detailed methodology, a clear update cadence, or a way to submit
corrections.

Explore it at [tow.cjr.org](https://tow.cjr.org/ai-deals-lawsuits/).
