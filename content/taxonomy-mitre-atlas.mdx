**MITRE ATLAS™** (Adversarial Threat Landscape for Artificial-Intelligence
Systems) is a globally accessible, living knowledge base of adversary tactics
and techniques against AI-enabled systems, drawn from real-world attack
observations and red-team demonstrations. It is modeled on the widely used MITRE
ATT&amp;CK framework.

### How it is structured

- **Tactics** — the adversary's goals (e.g. reconnaissance, model evasion,
  exfiltration).
- **Techniques** — the specific ways those goals are achieved against AI and ML
  systems.
- **Case studies** — documented real-world and demonstrated attacks that ground
  the framework in practice.

Explore it at [atlas.mitre.org](https://atlas.mitre.org/).
