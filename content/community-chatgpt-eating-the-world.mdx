**ChatGPT Is Eating the World** is a hybrid blog-and-tracker covering copyright
litigation against AI companies. Alongside dated news posts, it maintains
tracking tools such as an AI Copyright Case Tracker, an AI &amp; Authorship
Tracker, an AI Tort Lawsuit Tracker, and a master calendar of copyright suits —
documenting well over a hundred cases.

It sits in Community because it is primarily a news blog without a clearly
identified editorial team, documented methodology, or a structured way to submit
corrections and additions.

Explore it at [chatgptiseatingtheworld.com](https://chatgptiseatingtheworld.com/).
