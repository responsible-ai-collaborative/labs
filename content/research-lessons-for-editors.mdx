Curating an incident database is as much an editorial practice as a technical
one. This AAAI 2025 paper distills lessons from the people who maintain the **AI
Incident Database** — how to decide what counts as an incident, how to handle
ambiguous or contested reports, and how to keep classifications consistent as
the collection and its editors grow.

It is a practical companion for anyone building or operating a real-world AI
harm catalog.

Read it at [AAAI](https://ojs.aaai.org/index.php/AAAI/article/view/35163).
