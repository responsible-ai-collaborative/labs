The **Fisher Phillips AI Litigation Tracker** offers a view of litigation filed
across all 50 states involving AI technologies — generative AI, resume
screeners, electronic monitoring, notetaking, call recording, and more —
searchable by state, industry, and type of claim.

It sits in Community because, as presented, it does not document its scope,
sources, or methodology, show a clear update cadence, or provide a way to submit
corrections and additions.

Explore it at
[fisherphillips.com](https://www.fisherphillips.com/en/resources-and-innovation/trackers-and-maps/ai-litigation-tracker).
